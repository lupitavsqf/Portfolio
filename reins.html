Digital reins with haptic or tactile feedback for communication with non-verbal riders in the spectrum (Autism Spectrum Disorders). Made by BIG FROOTS in collaboration with GallopNYC.

GallopNYC is a therapeutic horseback riding program providing equine-assisted activities for children and adults with physical, emotional, and cognitive disabilities. Their mission is to foster growth, confidence, and independence through riding.

The Problem

Horse riding therapy lessons for neurodivergent individuals usually involve the assistance of one person to lead the horse and one or two people to assist the neurodivergent person get the instructions correctly. Some kids with autism (ASD) sometimes interrupt their lessons due to sensory sensitivities. For example, if the horse stops after walking for a bit, the interruption of the rythmic movements can cause some kids to feel uncomfortable and try to get off the horse, sometimes leaving them unable to finish the lesson.

Our solution: digital reins

BIG FROOTS' digital reins are an innovative assistive tool designed to support communication with non-verbal autistic riders through haptic and tactile feedback. By translating rider intent into subtle, responsive signals, they enhance interaction, focus, and engagement during riding sessions.

Following extensive research, we designed the rein holders, adding haptic feedback and hand warming technology to keep sensitive riders engaged even during stops, reducing the need to interrupt lessons. Besides helping riders with ASD, the reins result beneficial to give instructions to people with attentional problems or nonverbal. It also helps beginners learn how to keep the hands in the right position and receive haptic cues for instructions. And they help everyone keep their hands warm during winter.

Technical details

Radio technology: Transmits signals up to 3 miles away. Direct radio transmission enables millisecond-level response times, eliminating cloud-related latency and jitter.

Dual power options: Features a rechargeable lithium battery and a 9V battery receptacle for added reliability.

Adjustable hand grabbers with warmers: Attach securely to the reins, are fully adjustable for comfort, and provide support for beginners as well as warmth during winter.

Controls:

  • Ergonomic design: Small enough to be grabbed by one hand.
  • Left/right buttons: Send signals to the corresponding rein grabber.
  • Diamond button: Sends signals to both reins simultaneously.
  • Center heart button: Includes an on/off switch for heartbeat mode, a rhythm regulator, and two low-voltage red LEDs that blink as the respective grabbers receive signals.
